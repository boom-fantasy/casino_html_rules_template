Base Game

12 MORS as well as 3 Jackpots are displayed on the Matrix at all times.

Each bet placed offers a random chance to win one or more prizes from the displayed collection.

Bonus Feature

On any bet, one or more of three Bonus Feature Pots might randomly be impacted by a collect symbol:

  • If a meter is impacted, it may trigger a bonus with the associated feature of the Lock & Respin Bonus.
  • Prizes won on a triggering bet lock in place and are carried into the Lock & Respin Bonus.

Lock & Respin Bonus

The feature begins with 3 respins. Landing a new prize resets the respin count back to 3.

All positions without a locked prize respin independently, potentially awarding additional prizes or upgrades.

The Symbols on the reels consist of Money On Reels Symbols (MORS) and Jackpot Symbols

The feature ends when:

  • 3 consecutive spins result in no new prizes, or
  • All positions contain locked prizes.

Variants of Lock & Respin

  • Extra Spins: Start with 4 respins instead of 3.
  • Double Matrix: Play with two matrices; prizes are locked and respins occur independently for both.
  • Value Boost: Randomly upgrade the value of one or more locked prizes at the end of each respin. In the event of a Double Matrix and Value Boost bonus, Either or both of the Matrices can be affected by the Value Boost

Combination of Variants

It is possible to trigger multiple variants simultaneously. The bonus benefits from all triggered variants but plays only once. Possible combinations include:

  • Extra Spins
  • Double Matrix
  • Value Boost
  • Extra Spins & Double Matrix
  • Extra Spins & Value Boost
  • Double Matrix & Value Boost
  • Extra Spins, Double Matrix & Value Boost

Jackpots

There are 4 Jackpots, with values that scale with the bet multiplier.

Mini, Minor, Major Jackpots:

  • Appear on the main matrix and can be won during the base game or Lock & Respin Bonus.
  • Can be won multiple times during the bonus.

Grand Jackpot:

  • Awarded for filling all positions on a single matrix during the bonus.
  • If both matrices are filled during a Double Matrix Bonus, the Grand Jackpot is awarded for each matrix.

Straight to Bonus Feature Rules

Overview

The Straight to Bonus feature allows players to purchase direct entry into the Lock & Respin Bonus with a guaranteed number of Bonus Feature variants. The cost of the feature scales with the selected bet level and the number of guaranteed variants chosen.

How to Access

  1. Press the Straight to Bonus button located on the main game screen.
  2. Choose from one of the following purchase options:
    • 1+ Bonus Feature: Guarantees at least 1 Bonus Feature variant.
    • 2+ Bonus Features: Guarantees at least 2 Bonus Feature variants.
    • 3 Bonus Features: Guarantees all 3 Bonus Feature variants.
  3. Select your desired purchase option by pressing the BUY button.
  4. A confirmation window will appear showing the selected option and cost. Press OK to proceed.

The selected amount will be deducted from your balance, A bet will be placed that will award a Lock & Respin Bonus with the guaranteed number of Bonus Feature variants:

  • Extra Spins
  • Double Matrix
  • Value Boost

All standard rules of the Lock & Respin Bonus apply during the purchased feature.
