GAME RULES

Minimum Bet

30 credits.

Prize Values

18 prizes are available, displayed as the following amounts times the bet multiplier:

5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 50, 75, 100, 125, 150, 175, 250, 300, 500, 2000, 3000, 5000

Base Game

Each bet, two or more of the displayed prize amounts may randomly be awarded.

Prize Multiplier

Before two or more of the displayed prize amounts are randomly awarded, a multiplier between 2x and 10x may randomly be applied to all prizes, increasing their displayed amounts for the current bet.

Malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Incomplete games will automatically complete after 21 days.

Player is responsible for ensuring correct credits are registered before playing.
